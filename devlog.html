Building Elior’s Room

Goal: Create a room that feels innocent, personal, and quietly unsettling.

What I Did: Positioned the bed and teddy bear to be visible from the doorway, added furniture for function, and tested the space in play mode.

Challenge: The room initially felt too full, then too empty. I had to find the right balance.

What I Learned: Empty space can create tension when it feels intentional.

Mapping the Mansion

Goal: Build a layout that supports exploration and story progression.

What I Did: Planned major rooms including Elior’s room, the kitchen, living room, study, library, dining room, and bedrooms.

Challenge: Making the mansion feel large without becoming confusing.

Next Step: Continue refining room flow and begin adding environmental storytelling details.
